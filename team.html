Our Story

In a world where scientific progress saves lives every day, keeping biological materials safe is crucial.

They saw how poor storage could harm research and delay treatments. Inspired to make a difference, they created precise and reliable cooling solutions to protect these vital materials.

Founded in 2015, BioChill Technologies meet this need by preserving life-saving materials with advanced cooling technology.

Our story began with a dedicated team of engineers and biologists who knew how fragile vaccines, specimens, and medicines are.
